About SEACET

S.E.A College of Engineering and Technology (SEACET), established in 2007 by Founder Chairman Sri. A. Krishnappa, is a premier institution under the South East Asian Education Trust. Approved by AICTE and affiliated with VTU, SEACET offers a blend of modern and traditional education, fostering innovation and holistic development.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in technical education, nurturing innovation and ethical values.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, promote research, and develop skilled professionals.
